What is this?

This project is an iterative exploration of typography through HTML and CSS. The aim is to have a variety of 20 visual treatments that respond to the poem’s content. The text is an excerpt from Get Out of Bed by Diane Shore, which you can read below:

“Get out of bed, you silly fool!
Get up right now, it's time for school.
If you don't dress without a fuss,
I'll throw you naked on the bus!”

“Oh, Mom, don't make me go today.
I'm feeling worse than yesterday.
You don't know what I'm going through.
I've got a strange, rare case of flu.”

“You faked a headache yesterday.
Don't pull that stuff on me today.
Stop acting like a silly fool—
The principal cannot skip school!”

Each visual style category obeys a rule. In addition to the five rules, the last visual link is a reference to a pop culture movie keeping in theme with daydreaming, play time, study, skipping classes, and all things school.

Created by Shawki Izzat.
